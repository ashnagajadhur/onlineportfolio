Personal Brand Identity.

This logo has a traditional yet cotemporary style, which was inspired by the font Bella.

The colour palette chosen such as the black conjures up images of strength, wealth, and elegance, but it also conjure up images of professionalism. neutrality and simplicity. It is frequently employed to conjure up a sense of mystery. Yellow accents provide vitality to your design and make the viewer feel enthusiastic and happy. It's frequently utilised to pique people's interest in an energising and reassuring way. White is associated with minimalism and simplicity. A minimalist style is achieved by using a lot of white in design. which may result in a simple. fresh and clean look.
